How it works:

Characters: Each character has four features. Power, speed, stamina and mental. Users can has effect only on mental feature by their like or dislike. Characters show different behavior during the match based on thier features. Plyer with higher mental are the most favorite ones.

Roads: Each road has six different features. high and low steep slope, wave, dirt, soft and mountain. All of these features have different effect on each player.

Users: Users can rate all characters everyday and earn points based on their likes or dislikes. They can give 1 like or 1 dislike to each charact erevery day.
